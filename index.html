About Me

Born and raised in Eastern New Mexico & West Texas. Graduated Midland High School (1999). Received B.B.A. in Management from Texas Tech University (2002). Received MBA in Management from the University of Texas of the Permian Basin (2011). I love sports, backyard BBQ's, riding motorcycles & playing golf!

Have worked in the Travel Industry my entire professional career. Started with Amera Travel Network International (ATNI) as a Book Keeper (2003). Moved into and excelled in Travel Sales (2005) quickly becoming the Company's top six sigma producer. Promoted to General Sales Manager (2007). Promoted to International Operations as Business Development Manager (2009) and then President/CEO (2012-2020).
